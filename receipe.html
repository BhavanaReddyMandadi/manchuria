For 2 Servings: 20 pieces


    Step 1:
    • Take around 250 grams of listed chopped vegetables in a bowl
    • Mix them along with 4 tbsp of cornflour and 6 tbsp of all purpose flour
    • Add 2 tbsp of salt, 1 tbsp of green chilli paste, 1/4 tbsp of chinese salt
    • Mix gently together with water as per required to make into small balls
    Step 2:
    • Deep Fry these balls in oil until they become golden brown
    • Chop one onion and 3 green chillis into small pieces
    Step 3:
    • Take an another pan with 3 tbsp of oil
    • Add the chopped vegetables and fry them for 2 mins
    • For better taste add 1 tbsp of soya sauce, 2 tbsp of tomato sauce, and pour 1/4 cup of water
    • Boil it for 5 mins until it becomes thick and then add the manchurian balls in it
    Step 4:
    • Rest it for 2 mins and mix well and take into serving bowl
    • Garnish it with spring onions finely chopped and Serve Hot !!
